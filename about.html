Description

My inspiration for the website came from my interest of the depths of the ocean and the far reaches of space. I chose the ocean because there's so much of the ocean we have yet to explore. The ocean is filled with mystery and wonders. Space on the other hand is beyond our reach. Both the ocean and space are filled with mystery and wonders. I am hoping one can find the website calming and mysterious at the same time.

This is the logo I chose to represent my website. It has a space looking aesthetic.
